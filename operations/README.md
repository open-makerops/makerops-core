# Operations

Services used by operations and supply chain staff for inventory tracking, parts management, bill of materials, and purchase and sales order processing.

## Services

| Service | Port | Purpose | Idle RAM | Base Storage |
| ------- | ---- | ------- | -------- | ------------ |
| [InvenTree](https://inventree.org) | [8096](http://localhost:8096) | Inventory and parts management — stock, BOMs, purchase and sales orders | ~1.95 GB | ~140 MB |
